Hello There

We Are Goldstone Multiventures

Goldstone Multiventures Private Limited is a diversified enterprise driven by innovation, experience, and strategic leadership. Our core strength lies in garment manufacturing — from premium jeans and t-shirts to vibrant ethnic wear for men, women, and children. With a modern production setup and a dedicated team, we serve brands across India and globally. Through strategic import-export partnerships and quality-driven production, we aim to deliver value and sustainable growth in every project we undertake.

What We Do

We’ve got everything you need to build and grow your fashion business.

Garment Manufacturing

Jeans, t-shirts, ethnic clothing — we design and manufacture high-quality garments with end-to-end control.

Private Labeling & Branding

Custom brand tags, labels, and packaging for startups and retail chains. Build your fashion brand with us.

Import & Export

Global fashion logistics simplified. We manage documentation, sourcing, and compliance with ease.

Fabric & Material Sourcing

Sourcing from premium suppliers across India and abroad — denim, cotton, synthetics, and blends.

Custom Design Solutions

B2B Collaborations

Work directly with our manufacturing teams to scale your fashion brand or retail outlet.
